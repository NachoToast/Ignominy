Welcome to the world of Ignominy!

The year is 3051, but human civilization hasn't reached the technological epoch it once dreamed of.

Quite the contrary actually.

Humanity has regressed.

The global population is around 500 million, a mere fraction of what it used to be.

Technology, despite the many centuries that have elapsed since , is medieval at best,
and humanity's spot at the top of the food pyramid is no longer unchallenged.

Magical beasts from land, sea, and air are a never-ending threat to the safety of civilization.

Not everything is bad however.

Wild beasts aren't the only ones with magical powers.

Humans have also coevolved magical abilities of their own.

Although the term "humans" isn't really the same anymore.
